A sugar is typically a 5 carbon ring with an oxygen in the middle.

**The 5th carbon is outside the ring**

For [[Ribonucleic Acid]] the sugar used is [[Ribose]]

For [[Deoxyribonucleic Acid]] the sugar used is [[Deoxyribose]]
