Iron reacting with Water and/or Oxygen to create Iron oxides.

$$Fe_{(s)} + H_2O \ \text{or} \ O_2 = Fe(OH)3_{(s)}, \ FeO(OH)_{s} \ \text{or} \ Fe_2O_3\cdot H_2O_{(s)}$$
